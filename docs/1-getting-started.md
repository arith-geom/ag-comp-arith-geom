On the left sidebar, you will see a list of **Collections**. These are the different types of content you can edit:

*   **Home Page**: The main landing page.
*   **Research Page**: Research areas and descriptions.
*   **Publications**: List of papers, books, and software.
*   **Teaching**: Courses, seminars, and lecture notes.
*   **Members**: Profiles of current and former group members.
*   **Links Page**: Useful links for students and researchers.
*   **Contact Page**: Address, phone numbers, and email info.

## ğŸ’¾ Saving and Publishing

When you make changes, they are not immediately visible to the public. You must **Save** and then **Publish** them.

1.  **Make your changes**: Edit text, upload images, etc.
2.  **Save**: Click the **"Save"** button (usually at the top right). This saves your changes as a "Draft" or "Commit".
3.  **Wait**: It may take a few minutes for the website to rebuild and show your changes live.

> **ğŸ’¡ Tip:** Always double-check your spelling and links before saving!
